Syllabus

The syllabus and course is divided into three segments, as per the major aspects involved in robotics. There will be lectures on control, planning and perception, with projects and homeworks on the same.
There will be two examinations, 3 projects in multiple phases, and two homeworks.
This is a tentative syllabus and is subject to change.
